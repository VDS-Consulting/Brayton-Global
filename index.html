About Us

  • Who we are

    high-quality IT solutions since 2016

    Brayton Global was created in 2014, since 2016 our company is dedicated in supporting IT consultancy firms by providing them with high-quality IT solutions and services. Our mission is to help IT consultancy firms increase their efficiency and competitiveness by leveraging the latest technologies and industry best practices. Since 2019 we expended our services to the private sector, delivering consultancy in different domain such as Cybersecurity, Cloud Management, Field services but also providing customized software development and data analytics solutions. Our goal is to help businesses of all sizes improve their operations and stay ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving technological landscape.
