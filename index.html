Brayton Global Services is a company that specializes in providing a wide range of cloud-based services to businesses. These services include cloud infrastructure, cloud consulting, cloud migration, cloud security, cloud monitoring, and cloud management.

Cloud infrastructure services refer to the provision of computing resources such as servers, storage, and networking over the internet. Cloud consulting involves working with businesses to develop cloud strategies, assess their readiness for cloud adoption, and identify the best cloud solutions to meet their needs. Cloud migration involves moving data, applications, and other business processes from on-premises infrastructure to cloud-based infrastructure.

Cloud security services focus on protecting businesses' data and applications from cyber threats such as malware, viruses, and unauthorized access. Cloud monitoring involves tracking the performance and availability of cloud-based resources to ensure that they are meeting business needs. Cloud management involves the ongoing management of cloud-based resources, including monitoring, optimization, and cost control.

Overall, Brayton Global Services provides businesses with the expertise and support they need to successfully adopt and manage cloud-based infrastructure and applications.
