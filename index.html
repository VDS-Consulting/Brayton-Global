Looking at adopting and promoting a culture of recycling and waste reduction?

Gartner's research suggests that technology emissions from employees could account for as much as 73% of IT greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. Opting for refurbished or remanufactured materials is a key strategy for fostering a more sustainable IT landscape. At Brayton, we're committed to helping you integrate and maintain sustainable practices in your workplace through a circular economy model. Our services range from offering PCs-as-a-service to providing access to refurbished/remanufactured materials, alongside implementing strategies for spare parts harvesting and recycling. Partner with us to create a greener, more sustainable IT environment.

About Us

  • Who we are

    Established in 2014, Brayton Global has dedicated itself since 2016 to enhancing the European institutions market's IT service providers with on-demand services. Our goal is to elevate their operational efficiency and competitive stance using creative solutions and competitive pricing. Expanding our activities into the private sector in 2019 allowed us to broaden our offerings to include cybersecurity consultancy, cloud management, digital workplace services, custom software development and data analytics. Our mission is to assist businesses of every size in enhancing their operations and staying competitive in the rapidly changing tech landscape.
